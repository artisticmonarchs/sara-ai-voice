Enterprise FAQ

Strategic Questions,
Definitive Answers

Critical information for enterprise decision-makers evaluating Sara AI's capabilities and positioning.

Is Sara AI enterprise-ready today?
Sara AI is a production-grade system with core telephony, AI, and pipeline components fully operational. The SaaS packaging layer—self-serve onboarding, multi-tenant management, enterprise billing, and comprehensive UI—is the final phase that transforms this powerful internal system into a product operators can deploy without technical setup.
How does Sara maintain strict governance and avoid deviation?
Multiple governance layers ensure strict adherence: system prompts, conversation playbooks, objection handling flows, and knowledge boundaries are injected at every conversation turn. For maximum safety, a response-gating mechanism can block or regenerate any output that violates rules before text-to-speech conversion. This multi-layered approach ensures consistent, on-brand, and compliant conversations at scale.
What's the ideal use case for maximum ROI?
Appointment setting where the offer is clearly defined and the goal is single-step: book the qualifying call. This scenario provides the highest automation ROI with the lowest "creative ambiguity." Ideal for agencies, sales teams, and service providers with established offerings, clear value propositions, and systematic follow-up processes.
